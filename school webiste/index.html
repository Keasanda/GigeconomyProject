About Us

We've beem making healthy food last for 10 years

Welcome to L'Atelier, the premier destination for exquisite French pastries in South Africa. Located in Rosebank, Johannesburg, we bring the art of French baking to your neighborhood, offering a delightful selection of freshly baked bread, delicious pastries, and savoury sandwiches. Our skilled artisans use the finest ingredients to craft each item with care, ensuring an unforgettable taste experience. Complement your visit with our gourmet coffee, tea, or hot cocoa, all served in a beautifully designed space that combines elegance with comfort. At L’Atelier, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick coffee or enjoying a leisurely lunch, our goal is to make every visit memorable. Join us and indulge in a taste of France right here in Johannesburg!
